I am a research scientist at NVIDIA working on speech and multi-modal LLMs. Previously, I was PhD student at MIT CSAIL in the Spoken Language Systems Group, advised by Dr. Jim Glass. Before that, I graduated from MIT with my M.Eng. in EECS, advised by Dr. Jim Glass and Professor David Harwath. I started my journay at MIT with my S.B. in EECS, and I worked with Professor Antonio Torralba and Professor Josh McDermott.

During my PhD, I was part of the Sight and Sound MIT-IBM Watson AI Lab project. I was fortunate to work with Dr. Tatiana Likhomanenko at Apple's Machine Learning Research Group during summer 2023.
